My Piano Background

I was born in Orem, Utah and I grew up in Arizona. My family raised me singing and playing the piano. In high school, I competed in numerous competitions and evaluations, winning Honorable Mentions and placing in the EMTA competitions. I worked as an accompanist around the valley and saw the value of music in bringing people together.

I have continued my work in piano and I'm currently a piano teacher to over 25 students in Lehi, UT as I study Experience Design and Management. I have found so much value in creating a well-rounded and meaninful experience for my students!
